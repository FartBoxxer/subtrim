## Short description (150-200 chars)

## Medium description (300-400 chars)

Most subscription trackers just aggregate your bills. SubTrim goes further: you answer 3 questions per service (how often you use it, how much you enjoy it, would you miss it) and it tells you exactly what to cancel, keep, or downgrade. Spots overlaps between services. Outputs a shareable receipt showing your annual savings. No bank connection needed.

## Long description (600+ chars)

SubTrim is a free subscription auditor that goes beyond tracking. Unlike Rocket Money or Truebill, it doesn't just show what you're paying — it asks how you actually use each service and gives you a concrete recommendation: keep, cancel, or downgrade.

Features:
- Usage audit survey with keep/cancel/downgrade recommendations
- Overlap detection (flags when you're double-paying for the same thing — e.g., Spotify + Apple Music)
- Promo aggregator for active subscription deals
- Renewal calendar
- Household sharing (split tracking with roommates or family)
- Shareable savings receipt (export as PNG)

No bank connection required. Privacy-first: you enter subscriptions manually (takes ~60 seconds for 10 services). Free to use. Takes about 3 minutes to complete an audit.
